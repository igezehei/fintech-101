# Fintech 101: Navigating Stock Markets, Tariffs, AI, & 2025-2026 Global Economy

Welcome to **Fintech 101**, a comprehensive course designed to introduce you to the fundamentals of investing, stock market basics, and advanced strategies for 2025-2026. Learn how geopolitical shifts, U.S. tariff policies, global supply chain disruptions, and AI are reshaping financial markets in real-time.

## Features

- **Dynamic Navigation**: Navigate seamlessly between modules using the sidebar menu.
- **2025-2026 Market Context**: All modules include current examples of tariff impacts, geopolitical risks, and global economic shifts.
- **Interactive Content**: Each module includes key concepts, quizzes, case studies on tariff-impacted companies, and real-world activities.
- **PDF Export**: Generate a PDF summary of the course content with market analysis.
- **Data Security**: Hide sensitive data with a single click.
- **AI & Tariff Analysis**: Explore how artificial intelligence analyzes and predicts tariff impacts on stocks.
- **Shareable Links**: Easily share the course with others.

## Course Modules

1. **Module 1: Introduction to Investing (2025-2026 Edition)**
   - Learn the basics of investing and how geopolitical events, tariffs, and trade wars reshape investment landscapes.
2. **Module 2: Understanding Stocks & Tariff Impacts**
   - Understand how the stock market works, analyze trends, and see real examples of how tariff policies move markets.
3. **Module 3: Stock Market Basics in a Volatile Economy**
   - Explore foundational concepts and how supply chain disruptions affect different sectors.
4. **Module 4: Investment Strategies for 2025-2026**
   - Discover strategies to grow wealth while managing tariff-related volatility and geopolitical risks.
5. **Module 5: Risk Management in Uncertain Times**
   - Learn how to identify and mitigate investment risks, including tariff exposure and currency fluctuations.
6. **Module 6: AI in Stock Markets & Tariff Analysis**
   - Explore how artificial intelligence predicts tariff impacts, monitors supply chains, and identifies sectors most vulnerable to trade wars.
7. **Module 7: The Future of Investing**
   - Discover emerging trends like blockchain, DeFi, ESG investing, and how they interact with changing trade policies.
